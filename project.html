Project overview

The SSRMS platform will register and manage informal retail spaza shops throughout South Africa. It will provide registration, document upload, compliance scoring, inspection scheduling, and community-facing search and feedback features.

Mission

The mission is to create a fair, transparent, and efficient ecosystem for spaza shop operations that supports local entrepreneurship, ensures food safety, and facilitates regulatory oversight.

Features (short list)

  • Customer-facing search and reviews
  • Shop owner registration and compliance dashboard
  • Government inspector workflow and reporting
  • Map integration and nearby-shop discovery
